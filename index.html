✦ Runestone Rush ✦

A wizard. Ancient runes. One stone at a time.

Runestone Rush is a Sokoban-style puzzle game where you play a wizard relighting forgotten runes deep in a dungeon. Slide glowing rune-stones onto floor glyphs to light every rune and open the way forward — across hundreds of hand-tuned puzzles that grow from gentle to fiendish.
