🍎 Infruition 🥭

Pick perfect fruit, every time.

Infruition is a pocket guide to picking good fruit. For 40 of the most popular fruits in the United States, it shows you exactly what to look for and what to avoid at the store — how to read color, firmness, weight, and aroma; a ripeness timeline so you can buy to match when you’ll actually eat it; the red flags that mean it’s already gone bad; plus pro tips and the right way to store each one.

Every guide is built from real produce research and illustrated with clear, side-by-side “good vs. bad” photos. Browse by season, search by name, and save your favorites. Never bring home a mealy peach or a flavorless melon again.
