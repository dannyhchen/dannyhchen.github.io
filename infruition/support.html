Infruition

Infruition is a fruit-picking guide: for 40 popular fruits it shows what to look for and what to avoid at the store, how to read ripeness, spoilage red flags, storage tips, and clear “good vs. bad” photos. Need help or have feedback? We’d love to hear from you.

Contact us

Email danny.chen@gmail.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can. When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model and iOS version, and which fruit or screen it happened on.

Frequently asked questions

How do I use the app?

Open a fruit to see its full picking guide — what to look for, what to avoid, a ripeness timeline, spoilage red flags, pro tips, and storage. Use the Discover and All Fruits tabs to browse, search by name, and tap the heart to save a fruit to Favorites.

Where does the picking advice come from?

Each guide is distilled from reputable produce, extension-service, and culinary sources; the specific references are recorded per fruit in the app’s underlying research data.

Are my favorites saved?

Yes. Your favorites are saved automatically on your device. Deleting the app will erase them. The app works fully offline and does not require an account.

Why do I see ads?

Ads help keep the app free. Infruition shows a banner and occasional full-screen ads through Google AdMob. See our Privacy Policy for details on how advertising data is handled.

How do I manage ad and privacy settings?

On iOS you can review tracking and advertising options under Settings → Privacy & Security. In regions where consent is required, the app will ask before any personalized data use.
