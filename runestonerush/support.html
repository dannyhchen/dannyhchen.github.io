Runestone Rush

Runestone Rush is a Sokoban-style puzzle game: you play a hamster wizard escaping a dungeon by relighting its ancient runes — sliding glowing rune-stones onto the floor glyphs to set every rune alight and open the way out. Need help or have feedback? We’d love to hear from you.

Contact us

Email dannychen@gmail.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can. When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model and iOS version, and the level number where it happened.

Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Swipe to move the hamster wizard. Push every glowing rune-stone onto a floor glyph to light all the runes and complete the level. You can only push stones (not pull them), so plan ahead — a stone shoved into a corner can get stuck.

How do I undo a move or restart a level?

Use the on-screen buttons at the bottom of the puzzle to undo your last move or restart the current level from the beginning.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your progress is saved automatically on your device. Deleting the app will erase your saved progress.

Why do I see ads?

Ads help keep the game free. Runestone Rush shows a banner and occasional full-screen ads through Google AdMob. See our Privacy Policy for details on how advertising data is handled.

How do I manage ad and privacy settings?

On iOS you can review tracking and advertising options under Settings → Privacy & Security. In regions where consent is required, the app will ask before any personalized data use.

About the game

Runestone Rush was inspired by the author’s two children and their pet hamster, a determined little escape artist who kept working out how to open his cage. The author and his two kids made the game together, and it is dedicated to them.
