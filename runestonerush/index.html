✦ Runestone Rush ✦

A hamster wizard. Ancient runes. One stone at a time.

Runestone Rush is a Sokoban-style puzzle game in which you play a clever hamster wizard trapped deep in a dungeon. The only way out is to relight the ancient runes: slide the glowing rune-stones onto the floor glyphs to set every rune ablaze, unseal the next chamber, and inch your way toward freedom — across hundreds of hand-tuned puzzles that grow from gentle to fiendish.

Like all good Sokoban puzzles, you can only push the rune-stones, never pull them, so every move counts. Plan your escape one stone at a time.

The story behind the game

Runestone Rush was inspired by the author’s two kids and their pet hamster — a relentless little escape artist who kept figuring out how to open his cage and slip out for an adventure. The hamster wizard you guide through the dungeon is a tribute to that crafty, never-give-up spirit.

The author built this game together with his two children, who helped dream up the runes, the rodent hero, and the puzzles. It is lovingly dedicated to them.
